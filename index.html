FOR THE 1892-93 SEASON, THE TEAM FIRST ENTERED THE ENGLISH FIRST DIVISION, THEN THE HIGHEST LEAGUE IN ENGLISH FOOTBALL. IN 1902, THE NAME OF THE CLUB WAS CHANGED TO MANCHESTER UNITED FOOTBALL CLUB, AND IN 1908, THEY WON THE FIRST OF THEIR 20 ENGLISH LEAGUE TITLES. THEY THEN MOVED TO OLD TRAFFORD, THE CURRENT STADIUM, IN 1910.
